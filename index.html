ABOUT US

Huawei Technologies Services LLC has been in continuous operation since its establishment.The company has steadfastly set up a reliable market base. Due to its unfounded expertise on creating sturdy foundations on emerging markets within untapped areas. As a result we have managed to build up remarkable sales. Our main goal is to provide top notch, original and warranted products in combination with expert services which is inclusive of all rounded customer care services. HTSL has partnered with global and industrial leader brands who are strategic and they include the following: Samsung, Beats, Sony, Huawei, LG, HTC and Blackberry. Apart from our unparalleled service and after sales care, we provide quality, genuine and warranted products.

Our Mission

HTSL aims to be the leading distributor of the best electronic and mobile phone products in the market. We want to have a significant impact in the distribution industry by dedicating ourselves to providing high quality, original products that are warranted to the market through unmatched service and after sales care.

Our Vision

To be the world’s leading distributor within the cellular and electronic market globally. To achieve this, we are set and highly adaptable to rapid expansion through partnering with major brand companies. We also aim to be innovative and transparent in our services and always maintain ethics with those who we work with such as the manufacturers, the retailers and relevant partners.

OUR VALUED CUSTOMERS

Throughout the years HTSL has emerged to be one of the leading distributors of consumer products within Middle East and Africa. Currently we have established a good working relationship and entered into business agreements with more than 4000 market developers and retailers. Additionally, we strive to work closely with our partners such as telecommunication service providers, to establish, maintain and grow our consumer base. This is in conjunction with HTSL’s creative supply solutions and market outreach strategies. Some of the telecommunication service providers that we work with include, AT&T, T-mobile, verizon, Sprint. We also take into deep consideration upholding of our set work values and this has enabled us to maintain a stable and productive work environment both internally and in relation to our external partners. We aim to create and foster good working relationships with our customers based on excellent service delivery and trust. We are dedicated in providing highly individualized customer service along with fast and efficient delivery of products and seamless after sales support to our retail partners. We are an independent team, which means that we are not tied down to distributing only a particular range of products from a specific manufacturer or retailer. We ensure that your needs are satisfied by distributing a wide range of products at your convenience from a variety of manufacturers.
